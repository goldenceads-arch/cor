Q2. Is Skymarx Kharkhoda a good investment? ▼
Yes, it's a great investment option. It's close to large factories like Maruti Suzuki, UNO Minda, and others, which means rising job opportunities and growing demand for housing. Phase 1 selling out quickly shows strong confidence in the project.

Phase 1 received strong interest and initial development was completed. Phase 2 is currently under planning covering a larger area. Preliminary groundwork like road layout and site preparation has begun. The project is currently awaiting RERA registration.

Buyers who showed early interest in Phase 1 appreciated the pace of development and township layout. The response came from a mix of local families, investors, and professionals. Their positive feedback has contributed to growing interest in the next phase.

Q6. When will possession be given? ▼
The possession timeline will depend on project registration and development progress. This may vary depending on development progress and government approvals, but current work is on track.
